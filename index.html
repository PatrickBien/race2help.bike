Ride Hard, Impact Harder.

Our Mission

Welcome to race2help, where our passion for mountain biking meets a heartfelt commitment to community service. As a racing team specializing in MTB XC marathons, XCC races as well as the occasional Bike Park adventure, we're more than just athletes - we're agents of change.

Our team is inspired by the ethos of radelnundhelfen e.V., and we channel our energy and determination into not only conquering trails but also making a meaningful difference in the lives of those in need. Every race we participate in, every trail we master, we do it with a purpose: to raise awareness and funds for vital causes, supporting disadvantaged and needy children.

But race2help isn't just about racing and charity; it's about building a community. We encourage riders of all levels to join us, share in our passion, and contribute to a greater cause. Whether you're an experienced racer or a newcomer to the world of mountain biking, your participation helps to propel our mission forward.

At race2help, we believe in the power of community, the thrill of the ride, and the joy of giving back. Join us, and let's race to make a difference together.
