Ride Hard, Impact Harder.

Our Mission

Team race2help combines competitive mountain biking with community service. We participate in MTB XC marathons, XCC races, and bike park events while fundraising for disadvantaged children through our affiliation with radelnundhelfen e.V. We welcome riders of all experience levels to join our community-focused initiative.

How To Join Us

  1. Become Radeln und Helfen member
  2. Optional: order a discounted team outfit
  3. Register for races and events as: 'Team race2help.bike'
  4. Now: "Race Hard, Impact Harder." But: be nice!
